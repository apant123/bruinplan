earn unit credit for lower division coursework taken

outside the University of California. Transferable
courses may still receive subject credit and satisfy

degree requirements even if they do not earn unit
credit. Units earned from Advanced Placement,
International Baccalaureate, and GCE A-Level

examinations are not counted towards the 105 lower-
division unit totals. Please speak with an academic
advisor prior to taking coursework to discuss credit

you will receive.
