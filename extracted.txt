1)

COLLEGE REQUIREMENTS WHICH MUST BE TAKEN FOR A
LETTER GRADE
Effective Winter 2023, courses taken to satisfy

Writing I, Writing II, Quantitative Reasoning, and
Diversity requirements must be taken for a letter grade

and earn a C or better. Passed grades will no longer
satisfy these requirements for courses taken

Winter 2023 or thereafter.

2)

CONCURRENT ENROLLMENT RESTRICTION

You will NOT receive credit for courses taken elsewhere

(including UCLA Extension) while also enrolled at UCLA
during a regular term (Fall, Winter or Spring). You

should always consult your College counselor BEFORE

enrolling in courses at another institution. However,

students enrolled in a UCEAP Study Abroad program and

who have petitioned and been approved to concurrently

enroll in a UCLA Online course will receive concurrent
credit for the UCLA Online course.

3)

LOWER DIVISION STATISTICS COURSE RESTRICTION
You have completed a Lower Division statistics course.

Credit is allowed for only one course from Stats 10,

11, 12, 13, 15 (or former 10H, 11, or 14).

If you completed any combination of introductory

statistics courses at UCLA and another institution

prior to the 2019 Fall quarter, credit is only
granted for the first Introductory course

(excluding Economics 41, Life Sciences 40, Poli Sci 6,

or Pub Aff 60).

If you completed any combination of introductory

statistics courses at UCLA and another institution in

the 2019 Fall quarter or thereafter, a maximum of
8.0 units of credit will be granted (excluding Econ 41,

Life Sciences 40, Poli Sci 6, or Pub Aff 60).

Please note credit is granted in the order the courses

are completed.
